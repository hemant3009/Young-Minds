YOUNG MINDS Speak

This is a project to bring together budding Indian youth changemakers to provide them mentorship along with a platform to collaborate and work towards nation-building. We also work towards getting the voices of these young changemakers heard as we recognize the importance of the youth's perspective.

GOAL

The goal is to bring together budding Indian youth changemakers and thought leaders and provide them with mentorship, along with a platform to collaborate, and opportunities to contribute to nation-building. We also work towards amplifying the voices of young changemakers, recognizing the importance of youth perspectives in driving change.

Who Can Join?

  • Passionate about creating social impact
  • Individuals working on social impact or leading their own initiatives
  • Those with an idea and the intent to drive change
  • Age: 15 to 25 years
  • Must be an Indian citizen

Why Join?

  • Get mentorship from experts in your field
  • Connect with a nationwide network of changemakers
  • Collaborate to enhance impact and achieve collective goals
  • Share your ideas, experiences, and perspectives
  • Have your voice heard on important social issues
