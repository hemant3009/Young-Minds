SUMMIT

DETAILS

YOUNG MINDS Changemakers Summit 2024 was an amalgamation of 50 selected young changemakers from all across India and the world for a Summit, solely focused on solution-centric conversations and discussing the way forward.

The Summit took place in collaboration with and at the O.P. Jindal Global University, from 13th December to 15th December 2024.

WHY THE SUMMIT?

Various problems exist at the global, national and local level and as important is it to discuss them, we cannot emphasize enough on the importance of working on solutions. Solution-centric conversation often gets ignored and that is the gap the Summit wishes to fill. The Summit wishes to enable cross-sectoral interactions and lead a solution-focused discussion. The selected changemakers will not only be sharing their work and solutions of the problems they are working on, but also participate in other consultations, enabling cross-sectoral deliberation and discussion on a way forward.

THEME

Climate action

Gender and social equity

Poverty alleviation

Youth empowerment

Summit Include

Project Showcase (presentation of work by each changemaker).

4 Consultation sessions (on the 4 themes) in which all the changemakers participated.

Fireside Chat with Mr. William O'Connor, Deputy Counselor for Educational and Cultural Affairs, U.S. Embassy India.

Keynote Address by Shri Sujeet Kumar, Member of Parliament (Rajya Sabha).

2 Panel Discussions

  • Youth as Leaders of Today with speakers from YLAC, Pravah, One Future Collective & Nikore Associates
  • From Data to Impact with speakers from Ehsaas and The Leading You^nth

3 Workshops

  • Building Communication Strategies for a Social Change by Mr. N. Ramakrishnan
  • Dream, Aspire Achieve: Creating a Life you Love by Dr. Pankaj Gupta
  • Evaluation Methods for a Social Impact Project by Ms. Koyesha Mukherjee
